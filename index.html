🔐 Identity & Anti Buddy-Punch
Biometric Login (Face ID)
Worker must verify with device biometrics before clocking in. Cannot be faked by someone else.
PIN Fallback
Allow workers to use personal 4-digit PIN if Face ID unavailable. Disable for strict sites.
Supervisor Override
Master PIN lets supervisors approve clock-in when GPS or biometrics fail.
📍 Clock-In / Clock-Out & Geofencing
Clock-In / Clock-Out Module
Show the time-tracking panel. Turn off for roles that only need activity logs, not time tracking.
Auto GPS Geofencing
GPS tracking starts automatically when worker opens the app. Clock-in unlocks only when inside the site boundary. No manual "Check GPS" needed.
Auto Clock-Out on Exit
Automatically records clock-out when device leaves the site geofence. Timestamp & GPS coordinates saved.
Geofence Radius
Default 15m (≈ 16 yards). Adjustable down to 5m per site in Clients & Sites. The tighter the radius, the more precise the on-site detection.
📋 Logging Features
Camera Capture
Live camera opens directly in the app — take photos and record video without leaving.
Photo & Video Upload
Upload media from the device photo library as an alternative to live camera.
Voice Notes (Speech-to-Text)
Workers can dictate notes by voice instead of typing.
SMS & Email Reminders
Auto-send Twilio SMS and email reminders to workers who haven't submitted.
